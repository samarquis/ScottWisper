## Brand Guidelines
1. **Consistency**: Use the microphone symbol consistently across all touchpoints
2. **Color**: Maintain the green success color for primary actions
3. **Sizing**: Ensure logos remain readable at all sizes
4. **Accessibility**: Ensure sufficient contrast ratios
5. **Context**: Adapt logo presentation based on background (white/dark themes)
